Welcome to the Soul Relics Museum.
Soul Relics is a collection of personal objects that have held special significance to their owners during times of distress.
A Soul Relic can be anything of personal significance. It may have provided solace or comfort, helped recover a sense of self, or simply marked a time of difficult internal struggle.
As each object is unique, so is every narrative contained in these objects, and every person who chooses to share their story through them.
